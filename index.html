About
I am currently a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation.

I received my B.A. and M.A. in Linguistics at the University of Düsseldorf, where I worked under the supervision of Hana Filip. I also was a research assistant in the CRC 991 at the University of Düsseldorf (Project B01: "Verb frames at the syntax-semantics interface").

Research interests: semantics; pragmatics; scalarity; game theory; evolutionary linguistics; abstract algebra; focus; reference.
