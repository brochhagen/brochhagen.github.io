About

I am a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher (PhD student) at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation in Amsterdam. My research revolves around ambiguity and categorization, flexibility and convention, as well as economy and equivocation.
My project is a part of the ESSENCE network and my supervisor is Robert van Rooij.
