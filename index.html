About

I am a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher (PhD student) at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation in Amsterdam. My research revolves around ambiguity and categorization, flexibility and convention, as well as economy and equivocation. Currently, I am working on nominal modifier-head constructions & the emergence of (semantic) structure in signaling games.

My project is a part of the ESSENCE network (Evolution of Shared Semantics in Computational Environments) and my supervisors are Robert van Rooij and Ewan Klein.
