Hi, I'm Thomas Brochhagen

I'm currently a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation in Amsterdam. My research revolves around uncertainty in communication. More precisely, I deal with the question why we often leave to the unsaid what could have been said more explicitly. Relatedly, I ask why, rather than evolving toward more overt precision, natural language seems to foster ambiguity and underspecification.
